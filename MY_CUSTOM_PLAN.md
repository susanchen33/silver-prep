## ðŸ’¡ Key Principle

> **Don't just solve problems. CLASSIFY first, then solve.**

The goal isn't to solve 100 problems. It's to build the pattern recognition that lets you instantly know: "This is a binary search problem" or "This needs DFS."

**Quality > Quantity.** 

One problem with full analysis beats five problems solved by looking at hints.
